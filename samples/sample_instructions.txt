Assignment: Analytical Essay on Climate Change Solutions

Write a 500-800 word analytical essay examining current approaches to mitigating climate change. Your essay should:

1. Introduce the topic and present a clear thesis statement
2. Analyze at least THREE different climate change mitigation strategies
3. Evaluate the effectiveness, feasibility, and potential drawbacks of each strategy
4. Use evidence from credible sources to support your analysis
5. Compare and contrast the approaches
6. Conclude with a well-supported recommendation

Requirements:
- Length: 500-800 words
- Format: Standard essay format (introduction, body paragraphs, conclusion)
- Citations: At least 3 credible sources, cited in APA format
- Tone: Academic and objective
- Due: End of week

Grading will be based on content quality, organization, writing quality, adherence to requirements, and proper use of sources.
